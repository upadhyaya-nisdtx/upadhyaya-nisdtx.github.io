I created a project that simulated a parking garage to demonstrate my ability to use 5 different advanced data structures. This was inspired by real life parking garages. A line of cars was put into a Queue, the parking garage floors were separated into a Stack, the parking spots were mapped out by an Array, the validity of parking passes were in a Dictionary, and a Linked List filed the cars out of the garage.
